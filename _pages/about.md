I am a master's student in Mechanical Engineering, with research interests in **origami-inspired structures**, **flapping-wing aircraft**, and **mechanism design**.

My recent work includes the exploratory design of deployable aerodynamic surfaces for flapping-wing landing.

I aim to pursue a PhD in mechanical engineering, with a focus on intelligent and adaptive mechanical systems.
